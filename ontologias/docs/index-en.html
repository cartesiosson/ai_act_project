AI Act Unified Ontology (v0.40.0): Description back to ToC

Unified ai: ontology module integrating GPAI, contextual criteria, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, AIRO, Article 5 prohibited practices, Article 2 scope exclusions with override detection, and European Legislation Identifier (ELI). Complete coverage of unacceptable risk AI systems with direct EUR-Lex links. Semantic scope determination via ontology IRIs instead of hardcoded keywords. Optimized for forensic compliance gap detection with ontology-first approach.
